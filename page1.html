About Us

Our Story

Audrey Harvest Group was founded in 1918 and is now managed by the fourth generation. We maintain a century-long legacy of agricultural excellence, specializing in high-quality watercress and strawberries.

Audrey Harvest Group

Tradition and Innovation

We merge traditional farming methods with modern technology to ensure superior quality in every crop. In addition to watercress and strawberries, our expertise extends to a variety of herbs, specialty botanicals, and innovative watercress supplements that promote health and skin vitality.

Scale and Sustainability

Audrey Harvest Group has 46 acres of expanding fertile farmland.
We combine timeless farming techniques with modern innovations, producing 900,000 tons of fresh watercress annually for local markets and Singapore, making us Malaysia's largest watercress farm.

We are committed to sustainability, prioritizing environmental responsibility, and delivering produce that epitomizes freshness, flavor, and nutrition.
